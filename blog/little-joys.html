Little Joys

December 19 2020


1. Sprinting down the middle of the street to see the light exhibit at a nearby park that was closing in 10 minutes.

2. Singing throwback songs at the top of our lungs on a late night.

3. Learning how to cook curry with friends and enjoying the masterpiece (not really :p) of a dinner that we made.

4. Fangirling over an extremely well-written, concise, and eloquent proof written by a mutual acquaintance for one of our math assignments.

5. Eating a fancy sushi dinner and buying lottery tickets in celebration of a friend’s 18th birthday!


When life becomes overwhelming, as it inevitably will at some points, it's nice to slow down by remembering the little joys that occur in your everyday life.
