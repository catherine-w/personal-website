Why I Started a Blog

May 20 2020

Hello and welcome to my little writing corner:) Staying at home in quarantine has pushed me to try new things, so I decided to give blogging a shot. The purpose of this blog is to build a habit of doing rather than just planning. It’s through doing and making mistakes that I’ll eventually learn and grow.

What To Expect
- Some posts will track my journey of learning code!
- (starting fall 2020) updates on how university life is going
- stories and thoughts
- Whatever else I feel like writing about

Overall, this is just a space for me to create and share. I hope you stick around for a fun time!

Cheers,
Catherine
